Why is CubeCraze?
A: CubeCraze is designed especially for Malayalam and English cubing lovers. It offers tutorials and resources to help you learn and master puzzles like 2x2, 3x3, 4x4, and even the Pyraminx.
Can I upload and save my solve times on CubeCraze?
Yes! Once you log in, you can upload your solve times for puzzles like 2x2, 3x3, 4x4, and Pyraminx. Your username will be displayed next to your best recorded times so you and others can track your progress.
Is CubeCraze free to use?
Yes! CubeCraze is free to use, but you need to sign up and log in to access features like the cube timer, algorithms, and solver.
